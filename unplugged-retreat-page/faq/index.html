Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the unplugged retreat?

The best way to get to the Unplugged Retreat is on the magic school bus! Let your mind explore. Or you can I-15 to in Montana, past Butte and take exit 314.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Every team is right for the Unplugged retreat. Bonding is something that can strengthen team morale and break down walls to build up team creativity.

Is there really no wifi available?

Of course we have WiFi. This is the 21st century. ALthough we do have WiFi we strongly suggest taking the time to truly Unplug.

What size team can you accomodate?

We can accomodate a team between 5-20 people comfortably per group. We can accomodate more but please call to discuss the variables.
